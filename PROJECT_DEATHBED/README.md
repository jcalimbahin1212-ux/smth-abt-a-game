### Core Premise

> The atmosphere has mutated. Strange fractures of living light appear in the skyâ€”shifting, humming, moving with sentient intention. The Light is not evil. It is lonely. It attempts to communicate with humans, but its touch is lethal.

> Luis is the first victim who survives long-term exposure. Adrian, his twin brother, is his caretaker. Their bond is the emotional spine of this story.

> This is not a game about defeating a monster. It is a game about holding someone you love until the end.

---

### The Central Song

At the climax, Adrian sings to Luis as the Light overtakes him:

*"My story, my history*  
*I held the hope of being free*  
*The love that would hold me*  
*I promised care for thee*  
*I've told you I'm sorry*  
*I was just too careless..."*

---

### Design Philosophy

- **Caretaking over combat**
- **Intimacy over spectacle**
- **Tragedy without cruelty**
- **The Light is lonely, not evil**
- **Love persists, even when everything burns**

---

*"They didn't save the world. They didn't defeat the Light. They just loved each other until the end. And somehow, that mattered."*
